Round Robin Pairing

Pair Generator — No Repeats

Enter names (one per line), then generate every round. Each round pairs everyone up, and no two people are ever paired together twice — a full round-robin schedule generated with the circle method.

Players

10 players

How it works: with N players, a full round-robin covers every possible pair exactly once across N-1 rounds (N rounds if N is odd, since one player sits out — "BYE" — each round). This uses the standard circle method: fix one player, rotate the rest around them each round.
